About Me

Providing compassionate spiritual care through technology.

With over 15 years of experience in hospital and hospice chaplaincy, I bring deep spiritual care expertise to the digital space.

I am board-certified by the Association of Professional Chaplains and completed my Clinical Pastoral Education at major medical centers. My interfaith training allows me to serve people from all spiritual backgrounds with sensitivity and respect.

Recognizing the growing need for accessible spiritual support, especially for those who are homebound, in rural areas, or facing pandemic restrictions, I developed this telechaplaincy practice to extend compassionate care beyond physical walls.

My Approach

I meet you where you are spiritually, without agenda or judgment. Our sessions are confidential spaces to explore meaning, process difficult emotions, and draw on your spiritual resources for resilience.
